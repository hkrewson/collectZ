## Kavita Reading Progress Sync Contract

## Summary

This patch defines the first safe Kavita reading-progress sync contract. The contract keeps collectZ link-out/native-reader-first, identifies read-only progress visibility as the next viable implementation, and explicitly keeps progress writeback and embedded reading out of scope.

## What Changed

### Shipped

- Rechecked Kavita's upstream OpenAPI progress endpoints and documented the read/write boundary.
- Added a read-only Kavita progress contract helper.
- Added a fake-server progress contract probe that calls only `GET /api/Reader/get-progress`.
- Documented why chapter-as-issue rows are the first eligible progress rows.
- Kept progress writeback, KOReader sync shortcuts, embedded reading, page proxying, and background polling out of scope.
