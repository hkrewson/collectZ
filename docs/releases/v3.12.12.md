## Summary

This patch clarifies Dashboard Review drawer repair flow by labeling the lower editable details section as a manual fallback and adding short type-aware guidance.

## What changed

- Dashboard Review drawers now call the editable lower section `Manual fallback`.
- Missing-identifier drawers point users toward lookup first, then manual title or metadata cleanup when that is the better fix.
- Missing-cover drawers point users toward cover upload first.
- Sparse-metadata drawers keep the fallback fields open with concise guidance to add known descriptive data and leave uncertain values blank.
