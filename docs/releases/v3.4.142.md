# collectZ v3.4.142

- Version: `3.4.142`
- Status: `released`

## Summary

This patch makes Plex TV watched-state writeback episode-aware instead of blocking TV series outright.

## Changes

- Resolves Plex TV series writeback through `/library/metadata/:ratingKey/allLeaves` before calling Plex watched-state mutation endpoints.
- Allows explicit season-scoped Plex watched/unwatched actions from the TV season detail area.
- Writes Plex scrobble/unscrobble only against concrete episode rating keys.
- Updates local TV season watch state after successful episode writeback.
- Keeps broad TV import rewrites, automatic watched-state writeback, and non-explicit Plex mutations out of scope.
